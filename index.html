Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g pecorino cheese (grated)
  • 50g parmesan cheese (grated)
  • 2 cloves of garlic (peeled)
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions until al dente.
  2. Meanwhile, cut the pancetta into small pieces. Heat a little olive oil in a pan and cook the pancetta until crispy. Add the garlic and cook for another minute, then remove from heat.
  3. In a bowl, beat the eggs, then mix in the grated cheeses. Season with a little pepper.
  4. When the spaghetti is ready, reserve a cup of the cooking water and drain the rest.
  5. Quickly toss the spaghetti with the pancetta, garlic, and the egg-cheese mixture. Add a little cooking water if needed to create a creamy sauce.
  6. Serve immediately, with extra cheese and pepper on top.
